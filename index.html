Where Light Meets Glass

Each piece, a singular creation

At Sherman Art Works, we create handcrafted glass art and decorative Judaica, including Kiddush cups, silver-plated drinking horns, custom silver-plated shofars, handmade glass mezuzah cases, mezuzah cases crafted from natural horn and finished with silver plating, glass bowls, artistic glass trays, and many other unique handcrafted pieces. Each piece is individually handcrafted, making every creation unique and one of a kind. Custom orders are always welcome.
