How the numbers are computed (matches the workbook)
labor = hours × rate · silver = grams × spot · floor = (materials + packaging + labor + silver) × (1 + waste%) + overhead/piece · min price = floor ÷ (1 − margin% − channel%) · margin@current = (current − floor) ÷ current · current $ = current ₪ ÷ FX.
