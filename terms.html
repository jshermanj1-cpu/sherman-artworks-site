Sherman Art Works is a family-run handmade glass and Judaica studio based in Israel, operated by the Sherman family. You can reach us any time at shermanartwork@gmail.com or on WhatsApp at +972 52 348 2278.

Our Products

Every piece is made by hand in our studio. Small variations in colour, texture and form are inherent to handmade glass — they are what make your piece one of a kind, and are not defects. Product photos are representative; the piece you receive may differ slightly. Measurements are approximate.

Ordering & Order Acceptance

Orders are placed via WhatsApp or email — the cart on this site prepares a WhatsApp message for you; nothing is charged on the site itself. We may decline an order at our discretion — for example if a piece is no longer available — in which case anything you have paid is refunded in full.

Prices & Payment

Prices are set in Israeli Shekels (₪). US-dollar prices shown on the site are an indicative conversion at a live exchange rate and may differ slightly from the final amount, which is agreed with you before payment. Payment is currently by bank transfer or PayPal, arranged in the order conversation; a card checkout is planned. No payment is taken until you have approved the piece and the price.

Shipping

Shipping is free on every order, worldwide, with no minimum. All shipments are tracked and insured. Full details — dispatch times, delivery estimates, customs and what happens if a piece arrives damaged — are on our Shipping & Returns page.

Returns & Cancellations

Standard pieces can be returned within 14 days of delivery in their original condition. Custom and personalised pieces are non-returnable. Full conditions, including cancellation of orders, are on our Shipping & Returns page.
