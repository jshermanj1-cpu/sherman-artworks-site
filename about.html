Our Story

Three Generations of Glass

A grandfather's passion. A family's calling. A craft that keeps growing.

Where It All Began

A Love of Glass That Started with One Man

It began with a grandfather who could not pass a piece of glass without seeing something in it, the way it held light differently at every angle, the depth of colour you could coax from something that started as nothing more than sand and heat.

His passion became more than a hobby. It became a craft, then a workshop, then a legacy.

The Sherman Family

Three Generations, One Craft

I
First Generation
The Founder

Driven by a lifelong passion for glass and art, he built the foundation, developing techniques, discovering possibilities, and creating the first pieces that would define the family's style.

II
Second Generation
The Craftsmen

The craft was passed down hand to hand. The second generation grew up surrounded by glass, learning by watching and doing.

III

Today

The family workshop continues. Three generations of knowledge, technique and artistic vision come together in every piece.

The Craft

Not a Product. A Work of Art.

There is no mould, no template, no two pieces exactly alike. Each piece is shaped by hand using methods the Sherman family has developed and refined over three generations.
