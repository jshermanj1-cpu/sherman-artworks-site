Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

Sherman Art Works, Handmade Glass Judaica from Israel ("we", "us", "our") is a family-run handmade glass and Judaica studio based in Israel. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit shermanartworks.com, how we use it, and your rights.

Who We Are

Sherman Art Works is operated by the Sherman family, Israel. Our contact address is shermanartwork@gmail.com. We are the data controller for personal data processed through this website.

What Data We Collect

We collect minimal data. Specifically:

  • Analytics data - page views, session duration, country, device type, and on-site events (product views, WhatsApp clicks). Collected anonymously via Google Analytics 4.
  • Order and inquiry messages - when you contact us via WhatsApp or email, you share your name, phone number (WhatsApp), and the content of your message. We use this solely to respond to your inquiry and fulfil your order.
  • No account registration - this site does not require or offer user accounts. We do not store passwords, payment card numbers, or billing addresses.

Providing this information is voluntary and is not a legal obligation. However, if you do not provide your name, phone number, or message details, we will not be able to respond to your inquiry or fulfil your order.

Where Your Data Is Stored & International Transfers

Some of the personal data described above is processed and stored outside of Israel. Specifically: order and inquiry messages sent via WhatsApp are processed on servers operated by Meta Platforms, Inc. (United States); emails are stored by Google LLC (Gmail) on servers located in the United States and/or the European Union; and anonymised analytics data is processed by Google Analytics (United States). Where personal data is transferred abroad, we rely on the receiving providers' own data-protection safeguards, in accordance with the Protection of Privacy Regulations (Transfer of Data to Databases Abroad), 5761-2001.

Google Analytics 4

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use our site. GA4 sets first-party cookies on your browser to distinguish sessions and track anonymised behaviour. No personally identifiable information is sent to Google.

You can opt out of GA4 tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on, or by using a browser with tracking protection enabled.

Cookies

This site uses the following cookies:

  • _ga, _ga_* (Google Analytics) - anonymous analytics, expire after 2 years / 24 hours respectively.
  • sa_lang, sa_cur (Sherman Art Works) - remember your language and currency preference. Session-scoped, stored in localStorage, never sent to any server.
  • sa_cart (Sherman Art Works) - remembers items you have added to your cart. Stored in localStorage. Never sent to any server.

We do not use advertising, retargeting, or third-party tracking cookies.

WhatsApp Ordering

When you click "Order on WhatsApp", you are redirected to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message containing your selected items. Your WhatsApp number and message are processed by WhatsApp (Meta Platforms, Inc.) under their own privacy policy. We receive and read your message to process your order, and we retain our WhatsApp conversation history in line with normal business records.

Email Contact

Emails sent to shermanartwork@gmail.com are stored in Gmail (Google LLC) and retained for as long as necessary to handle your enquiry. We will never share your email address with third parties.

Images & CDN

Product and studio photographs are served via Cloudinary (Cloudinary Ltd, Israel/US). Images are cached on Cloudinary's global CDN. Cloudinary may log request metadata (IP address, timestamp) in line with their own privacy policy. No personal data you provide to us is shared with Cloudinary.

Data Security Measures

We take reasonable steps to protect the personal data we handle. All traffic to and from this website is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. This website does not collect or store payment card numbers, CVV codes, or billing details. Order and inquiry data received via WhatsApp or email is accessible only to the Sherman family members who operate this business, and is not stored on any servers we manage ourselves - it resides with Meta Platforms and Google LLC respectively, both of which maintain their own security programs. These measures are intended to comply with the Protection of Privacy Regulations (Data Security), 5777-2017, as applicable to a database of this size and sensitivity.

Your Rights (GDPR & Israeli Privacy Law)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or another jurisdiction with data-protection law, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority

Israeli residents are also protected by the Protection of Privacy Law 5741-1981 and its regulations.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at shermanartwork@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained in Google Analytics for 14 months (our configured retention window). WhatsApp and email correspondence is retained for as long as necessary to fulfil orders and handle after-sale queries, typically up to 3 years. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact
