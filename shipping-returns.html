Shipping & Returns

Last updated: June 2026

Every order ships free, worldwide, tracked and insured — and is gift-wrapped before it leaves the studio. This page covers dispatch and delivery times, customs, damage, returns and cancellations.

Free Worldwide Shipping

Shipping is free on every order, to anywhere in the world. No minimum order, no hidden fees. Each piece is packed by hand in protective materials and gift-wrapped at no extra cost.

Dispatch & Delivery Times

Ready pieces ship from Israel within 2–3 business days of order confirmation. Worldwide delivery typically takes 7–14 days depending on destination. Custom and made-to-order pieces are designed, made and shipped within 2–4 weeks — we agree the timeline with you upfront and keep you posted as your piece progresses.

Tracking & Insurance

Every shipment is sent with full tracking and is insured door to door. You receive the tracking number as soon as your piece ships.

Customs & Import Duties

We ship from Israel. Depending on your country, your order may be subject to import duties, taxes or customs fees on arrival. These charges are set by your local customs authority, are not included in our prices, and are the recipient's responsibility. Many destinations apply no charge to low-value personal imports — your local customs website will have specifics.

If Your Piece Arrives Damaged

Everything is packed with great care, but glass travels the world — and very occasionally things happen. If your piece arrives damaged, send us a photo within 7 days of delivery and we will replace it free of charge, including shipping. If an identical replacement isn't possible (many pieces are one of a kind), we'll offer an equivalent piece or a full refund — your choice.

Returns

Standard pieces can be returned within 14 days of delivery, provided they are unused and in their original condition and packaging. To start a return, message us on WhatsApp or email us — we'll confirm the return address and walk you through it. Return shipping is arranged and paid by you, and we recommend a tracked, insured service. Once the piece arrives back safely, we refund the full item price to your original payment method within 14 days.

Non-Returnable Items

Custom and personalised pieces — anything engraved, name-bearing or made to your specification — cannot be returned or exchanged. That is exactly why we work so closely with you during the design phase, and take no payment until you have approved the design and price.

Cancellations

Standard orders can be cancelled free of charge any time before dispatch. Custom orders can be cancelled free of charge before crafting begins; once work on your piece has started, the order can no longer be cancelled. Since payment is only taken after you approve the design, you will never be charged for something you didn't confirm.

How to Start a Return or Claim

Message us on WhatsApp at +972 52 348 2278 or email shermanartwork@gmail.com with your order details (and photos, for damage claims). We respond personally, usually within one business day.
